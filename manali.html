About this tour

Manali, nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, is a haven for adventure seekers and nature lovers. From snow-capped peaks and lush valleys to vibrant markets and ancient temples, Manali offers a perfect blend of excitement and tranquility.

Included/Excluded

  1. ✅ Pick-up and drop-off from Chandigarh/Delhi Airport.
  2. ✅ 3-star accommodation with breakfast and dinner.
  3. ✅ Guided sightseeing tours as per itinerary.
  4. ✅ All transfers and local transport.
  5. ❌ Flight/Train Charges
  6. ❌ Personal expenses

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Arrival in Manali

    Arrive at Manali and transfer to your hotel. Rest and acclimatize to the altitude. Evening free for leisure.

  2. Day 2 - Manali Local Sightseeing

    Visit Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, and explore the Mall Road.

  3. Day 3 - Solang Valley Adventure

    Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and ropeway in Solang Valley.

  4. Day 4 - Rohtang Pass Excursion

    Drive to Rohtang Pass (subject to permit and weather). Enjoy snow activities and breathtaking views.

  5. Day 5 - Naggar Castle & Art Gallery

    Visit Naggar Castle, Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery, and enjoy riverside walks.

  6. Day 6 - Kullu & Manikaran

    Excursion to Kullu Valley and Manikaran Sahib. Enjoy river rafting and visit local temples.

  7. Day 7 - Departure

    Check out from hotel and transfer to airport/railway station for onward journey.
