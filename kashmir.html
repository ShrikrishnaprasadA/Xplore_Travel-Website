About this tour

Kashmir, also referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is an enchanting region that boasts picturesque landscapes, pristine lakes, lush green valleys, and vibrant gardens, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and peace seekers alike.

Included/Excluded

  1. ✅ Pick-up and drop-off from Srinagar Airport.
  2. ✅ 3-star accommodation with breakfast and dinner included.
  3. ✅ Guided sightseeing tours as per the itinerary.
  4. ✅ One night stay in the houseboat
  5. ❌ Flight Charges

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Arrival in Srinagar

    Welcome to Srinagar!

    Upon arrival at the airport, you will be greeted by our representative and escorted to your hotel.

    Experience the unique charm of Kashmir by staying in traditional Kashmiri Houseboats, floating gracefully on the serene waters of Dal Lake.

    Admire the intricate local interiors and unwind amidst the tranquility of your surroundings.

    Overnight stay at the houseboat in Srinagar.

  2. Day 2 - Srinagar Sightseeing

    Start your day with a delicious breakfast at the hotel.

    Visit the famous Mughal Gardens, including Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.

    Explore the local markets and shop for souvenirs.

    Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  3. Day 3 - Excursion to Pahalgam

    After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Pahalgam (approximately 2 hours).

    Visit the Betaab Valley, known for its picturesque beauty and Bollywood film shoots.

    Take a leisurely walk along the Lidder River and enjoy the stunning views.

    Return to Srinagar in the evening and enjoy dinner at the hotel.

  4. Day 4 - Gulmarg Excursion

    Start the day with breakfast at the hotel.

    Drive to Gulmarg (approximately 1 hour) and take a cable car ride to the top of the mountain.

    Enjoy skiing, snowboarding, or simply take in the breathtaking views.

    Return to Srinagar in the evening and enjoy dinner at the hotel.

  5. Day 5 - Departure from Srinagar

    After breakfast, check out of the hotel and proceed to the airport for your onward journey.
