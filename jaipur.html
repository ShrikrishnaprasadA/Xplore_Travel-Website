About this tour

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famous for its majestic forts, palaces, and colorful markets. Experience the grandeur of Amber Fort, City Palace, and the vibrant culture of the Pink City.

Included/Excluded

  1. ✅ Airport/railway station transfers
  2. ✅ 3-star accommodation with breakfast
  3. ✅ Guided sightseeing tours
  4. ❌ Entry tickets to monuments
  5. ❌ Personal expenses

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Arrival & City Palace

    Arrive in Jaipur, visit City Palace and Jantar Mantar.

  2. Day 2 - Amber Fort & Hawa Mahal

    Explore Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and local markets.

  3. Day 3 - Nahargarh Fort & Shopping

    Visit Nahargarh Fort, shop for handicrafts.

  4. Day 4 - Departure

    Check out and transfer for onward journey.
